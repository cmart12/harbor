Welcome to whim

A few things that make whim fast. Reopen this anytime with the ? in the header.

  • ⌨️
    Summon whim from anywhere
    Press ShiftSpace to pop whim open, capture a thought, and dismiss.
  • 🎙️
    Capture by voice
    With the box empty, press Space to record. Transcription runs locally on your machine.
  • 🤖
    Put agents to work
    Open a canvas, select text, and @mention a persona to launch an agent on it.
  • 📄
    Open a canvas
    Click a space (or press Enter) to open its rich markdown canvas.

When unchecked (default), comments only open when your cursor moves into a commented selection. Commented links and resource pills still respond to hover.
Export Destinations
Push a copy of a canvas (PDF, Word, or Markdown) into a folder for quick access and sharing. Point these at folders synced by OneDrive/SharePoint or Google Drive desktop apps and your exports show up in the cloud — and on your phone — automatically.
